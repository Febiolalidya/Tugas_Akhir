About Me

Hello friends! This exciting game was created by Febiola Lidya Sianturi, who has some crazy ideas for playing while learning. All the characters, images, and stories in this game are created with passion to keep you entertained and curious to continue exploring this game world.

Every button, color, and animation in this game is designed to be easy to use and fun. You can press the buttons easily, watch the characters move cutely, and discover little surprises in each level. Each challenge is fun yet manageable, so you'll feel happy and proud every time you succeed.

The music and sound effects in this game were carefully chosen to make every moment feel lively and exciting. From the cheerful sounds, to the chuckles that occur during some surprises, everything is designed to make you smile and laugh. Even the funny sounds will make you even more curious and excited to play. Every sound effect is designed to make you feel like you're truly in the game world, full of color, surprises, and joy.

Thank you so much to all the friends, teachers, and family who supported and provided fun ideas for this game. We hope everyone who plays has fun, learns new things, explores the world of their imagination, and keeps smiling. Remember, this exciting adventure is made especially for you!
